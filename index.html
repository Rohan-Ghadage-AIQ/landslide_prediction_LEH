Satellite Datasets Used

This system integrates multi-spectral optical imagery, digital terrain elevation models, dynamic rainfall triggers, and official GSI ground-truth landslide records:

  • SRTM 30m DEM (USGS/NASA): Extracts elevation, slope gradient, and aspect orientation.
  • Sentinel-2 (ESA): Calculates 10m Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI).
  • CHIRPS Rainfall (UCSB): Monitors precipitation and ground saturation triggers.
  • GSI NLSM Repository: Verified historical landslide coordinates for model calibration.
